Common Inquiries

Quick answers to frequently asked questions about starting a project with Wrapt.

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines vary depending on complexity, but most structural and graphic design projects are completed within 2-4 weeks from discovery to final delivery.

Do you handle print production?

While we focus on design and 3D mockups, we provide print-ready files and can recommend trusted production partners to ensure your design translates perfectly to the final product.

What are your project rates?

We offer competitive project-based pricing tailored to your specific needs. Contact us with your project details for a custom quote within 24 hours.
