Common Inquiries

Do you handle the actual printing?

I provide production-ready files and can manage the relationship with your chosen printer to ensure the final product matches the design vision perfectly.

What is the typical turnaround time?

A full packaging project typically takes 4-6 weeks from discovery to final files, depending on the complexity of structural design.

Can you redesign existing packaging?

Absolutely. Redesigning legacy brands for modern retail is one of our specialties. We focus on maintaining brand equity while maximizing shelf impact.
